Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What We Collect

We collect WhatsApp phone numbers, product information shared by sellers, and conversation history to provide our service.

2. How We Use It

Data is used solely to operate the Flux commerce assistant — listing products on Shopee and Lazada, generating marketing content, and notifying sellers of orders.

3. Third Party Services

We use WhatsApp Business Cloud API (Meta), Shopee Open Platform, Lazada Open Platform, and Anthropic Claude API to deliver our service.

4. Data Retention

Conversation sessions are retained for 24 hours. Product listings are retained until the seller requests deletion.
